Favorite Boulder Rides

Here are a few local rides I love close to Boulder across multiple diciplines (MTB, ROAD, GRAVEL).

1) Super Betasso MTB

★★★★★ Smooth flow with punchy climbs.

  • Distance: ~30 km
  • Best time: Evening golden hour
  • Why it’s great: Flowy descents with tight corners and technical descent to the bottom.

2) Flagstaff Road

★★★★★ A classic Boulder climb with rewarding views of the Flatirons.

  • Distance: ~20 km
  • Elevation gain: 1000+ m
  • Why it’s great: Great workout and an amazing climb right outside of Boulder. Such a fun descent back into towm.

3) Switzerland Trail Gravel

★★★★★ Historic railroad grades with big Front Range views.

  • Surface: Chunky gravel / 4x4 road
  • Bike: 40–50c tires recommended
  • Why it’s great: Technical gravel ride in the front range with great views. Bonus: climb up to goldhill for an amazing home made cookies at the general store.
